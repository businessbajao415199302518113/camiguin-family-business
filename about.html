JB Eatery

Founded by Jose Arnel Bajao, JB Eatery offers a wide variety of home-cooked Filipino dishes. It's best known for its special Bulalo, RM (Remember Me), and Balbacua. With dine-in, take-out, and delivery options, JB Eatery continues to serve the community with love and flavor.

J&A Transportation

Owned and operated by Jose Arnel Bajao, J&A Transportation is a trusted transport service provider in Camiguin Island. With 9 multicabs, 4 motorcycles, and 1 Toyota Innova, the business supports local travel with safety and reliability.

Marilyn Store

Marilyn Store is a mini sari-sari store managed by Marilyn Bajao. Located in Purok 2 Magting, Mambajao, Camiguin, the store offers everything from rice to everyday essentials. It serves as a convenient stop for the neighborhood and nearby travelers.
